# MVP Scope

The MVP should prove that customers can discover beauty vendors and request appointments.

## Customer MVP

- Signup and login.
- Browse vendors by city and category.
- Search vendors.
- View vendor profile.
- View services and prices.
- View gallery photos.
- Open direct call.
- Open WhatsApp chat.
- Open Google Maps navigation.
- Send a booking request.
- View own bookings.

## Vendor MVP

- Submit registration data.
- Maintain business profile.
- Manage service list and prices.
- Manage gallery photos.
- View booking requests.
- Update booking status.

## Admin MVP

- Approve or reject vendors.
- Manage vendor categories.
- Manage cities and areas.
- Feature selected vendors.
- Moderate reviews later in the MVP if reviews are included.

## Not In First MVP

- In-app payments.
- Advanced staff scheduling.
- Complex loyalty points.
- Real-time chat.
- Multi-branch enterprise vendor tools.
- Full analytics dashboard.
