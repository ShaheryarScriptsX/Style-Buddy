# Vendor Onboarding

Vendor onboarding collects all information needed to publish a professional salon or beauty business profile.

## Required Data

- Salon or business logo in HD PNG or JPG.
- Complete business name.
- Full address.
- Area.
- City.
- Contact number.
- WhatsApp number.
- Official email address.
- Instagram link.
- Facebook page link.
- Website link, if available.
- Services list with prices.
- 5 to 10 salon, interior, service, or work photos.

## Approval Flow

1. Vendor submits registration data.
2. Admin reviews business details, images, and contact information.
3. Admin approves or rejects the vendor.
4. Approved vendors appear in customer search and listing screens.
5. Rejected vendors receive a reason and can submit corrections later.

## Profile Quality Rules

- Business name must be clear and professional.
- Contact number must be valid.
- Address should include area and city.
- Services should include prices.
- Gallery photos should represent real salon work or location.
- Social links should be valid URLs when provided.
